Overview of how to play

  • LEFT KEY: the brick block will move to the left.
  • RIGHT KEY: the brick block will move to the right.
  • DOWN KEY: the block blocks will move down under.
  • UP KEY: the block will change direction.

    • Level 1

  • The player stacks blocks of bricks to fill at least 1 row so that the row disappears.

    • Level 2

  • The game will appear an additional obstacle that will continuously run back and forth and when the block touches the obstacle, the game will end, you will receive a defeat message on the screen.

    • Level 3

  • Every 15s the Board will automatically generate a bottom line, which means the space on the board is also narrowing.
  • The player's task is to arrange the blocks as quickly as possible so as not to lose.

    • Level 4

  • You will control a block of bricks located in the top row, the Board game will automatically generate more bottom lines that will cause the lines to be pushed up.
  • The game will end when the board is full to the top row.
    • LEFT KEY: the brick block will move to the left.
    • RIGHT KEY: the brick block will move to the right.
    • DOWN KEY: the block blocks will move down under.

    Level 5

  • Blocks after falling to a certain position will be moved to a new location in the game.
  • In this game screen, the player also has a special brick block, when it hits the ground it will delete the row above it, below it and the row where it landed.

    • Level 6

  • This play screen has a size of 20X20 cells, once playing will randomly appear obstacles. You have the task of destroying those obstacles by lining up the bricks so that they are in the same row with the obstacles. When that row is filled it will remove the obstacle as well. With each row cleared, the obstacles will gradually move down.
  • After destroying all obstacles you will win.
